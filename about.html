About

A dynamic mechanical engineering graduate from Mumbai University with a proven academic record and a passion for cutting-edge research and innovation. With experience in additive manufacturing (AM), biomedical engineering, and opto-mechanical systems, I have cultivated my research skills through technical internships at renowned institutions like ISRO and IIT Bombay.

I have contributed to projects ranging from space exploration satellites to smart biomedical devices and multi-axis extrusion based AM. I am a versatile researcher, committed to pushing boundaries in engineering, for continuous research and sustainable development. I am currently seeking direct-PhD positions to pursue my interest in convergent manufacturing and evolve myself as a mature researcher and make a meaningful impact in the society.
